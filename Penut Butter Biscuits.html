Peanut Butter Biscuits

  1. Home
  2. Recipes
  3. Recipe Request

Ingredients

  • 110g marge
  • 2 Table Spoon Penut Butter
  • 5ml Vanilla
  • 1/2 tspoon bicarb
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups flour

Instructions

Be sure to preheat the oven to 180℃

  1. Cream marge & sugar
  2. Add egg, penutbutter & vanilla
  3. Add flour & bicarb
